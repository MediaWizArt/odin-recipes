Veggie Burger

Description

Tasty bean and potato based vegetarian burger with crisp fresh vegetables and tasty and crunchy fries on the side.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Veggie patty
  • Optional - Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Potatoes to fry
  • Dressings

Steps

  1. Heat up your oil and your stove.
  2. Put fries in the oil and burger patties in the stove with some parchment paper
  3. Take your vegetables and slice the tomato and onions, and cut a piece of lettuce to fit your bun. Make sure to wash them before.
  4. When there's two minutes left on both the burger and the fries, lightly toast your buns and start assembling the base of the burger
  5. Optional - Put your cheese under where the patty will go, as well as on top so it lightly melts
  6. Remove the fries from the oil and place them on a piece of kitchen paper to absorb the excess oil - now is a great time to season them to taste aswell
  7. Place your patty on in the burger
  8. Enjoy!
